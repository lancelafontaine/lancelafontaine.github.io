About Me

My name is Lance Lafontaine and I am a software engineering student at Concordia University with a previous degree in molecular biology. You can read all about my recent change in career paths here, but TLDR: I've discovered that being a developer is just so much more gratifying.

I love all things web dev, Linux and open source, but I also like other non-techy things, like camping, music, and coffee.

On this online home of a website, you'll find a lot of information about me. I write blog posts about things. I link to things like my GitHub, StackExchange and StackOverflow accounts. I even have my qualifications and accomplishments here in case they might interest you.

I like to think of myself as a rather sociable guy in both virtual and physical settings, so don't hesitate to reach out to me on Twitter, or to connect on LinkedIn. You can also count on an email reply rather quickly.
